<h4>Capable coverage, comfortable protection</h4>
The Starvos MIPS road helmet offers ample protection, airflow management and comfort with the widest range of head sizes. A clear choice for cyclists of all levels.

MIPS (Multi-directional Impact Protection System) was developed by brain surgeons and scientists to help reduce rotational forces on the brain caused by angled impacts to the head.
