<h4>Ready for the rough stuff</h4>
Quantum is a lightweight, breathable mountain bike glove that keeps you covered when trail debris or overgrown vegetation attacks. Built with a synthetic leather palm, Quantum offers great durability and excellent handlebar feel to always keep you in control. Quantum also has plenty of back of hand protection for when things get hairy out there.
